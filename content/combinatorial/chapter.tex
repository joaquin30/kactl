	\section{Catalan Numbers}
		$$C_n=\frac{1}{n+1}{2n \choose n} = {2n \choose n}-{2n\choose n+1}$$
		$$C_0=1, C_{n+1}=\sum C_iC_{n-i}$$
		First few are $1, 1, 2, 5, 14, 42, 132, 429, 1430, 4862$.
